# Template
_Makes 4 large portions_

## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp cocoa powder
- 1 tbsp dark rum
- 1 tbsp amaretto
- 1/2 cup espresso 
- 1/2 tbsp vanilla extract
- 3 medium cold pasturized egg yolks
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1/8 tsp salt
- 8oz cold mascarpone
- 7/8 cup heavy cream
- ladyfingers

## Instructions
1. Whisk cocoa powder, alcohols, espresso, & vanilla
2. Beat egg yolks + sugar until pale/thick
3. Add salt + mascarpone, whip until smooth
4. Add cream, whip until creamy & medium peaks
5. Dip ladyfingers to make layers: ladyfingers, cream, cocoa powder, ladyfingers, cream, cocoa powder
