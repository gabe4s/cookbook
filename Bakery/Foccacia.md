# Template
_Makes 1 bread_

## Ingredients
- 3.5g Instant Yeast
- 280g 00 Flour
- 235g Water
- 6g Salt
- 1tbsp Olive Oil

### Extra
- Butter + Oil For Pan

## Instructions
1. Mix all ingredients
2. Rise for 30 mins, stretch dough (4x)
3. Coat pan in butter + oil
4. Pour dough out onto oiled pan
5. Rest 1 hour
6. Shape and dimple
7. Top with salt
8. Bake @ 400f 20-25m
