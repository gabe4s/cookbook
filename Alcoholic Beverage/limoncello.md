# Limoncello
_Makes ~650ml_

## Ingredients
- 250ml 190 Proof Everclear
- 3 Medium Meyer Lemons
- 183g sugar
- 360ml quality water

## Instructions
1. Steep lemons in everclear for 2 weeks
2. Create simple syrup
3. Add warm simple to extraction and chill
