# Limoncello
_Makes ~650ml_

## Ingredients
- 250ml 190 Proof Everclear (200g)
- 4 Medium Meyer Lemons (35g peels)
- 183g sugar
- 360ml quality water

## Instructions
1. Steep lemons in everclear for 3 weeks
2. Create simple syrup
3. Add warm simple to extraction and chill

## Notes
- Last batch was 208g of everclear, 208g of everclear =~ 260ml of volume
- 30g of lemon peels
