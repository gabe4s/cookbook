# Cacio E Pepe
_Makes 2 medium portions_

## Ingredients
- 250g Semolina
- 105g Water
- 45g Pecorino Romano
- 20g Parmigiano Reggiano
- 100g Guanciale
- Black Pepper
- Reserved Pasta Water

## Instructions
1. Make pasta
2. Crisp guanciale
3. Cook pasta
4. Reserve pasta water
5. Strain pasta
6. Add pasta to guanciale fat
7. Stir in cheese, a little pasta water, pepper to taste
